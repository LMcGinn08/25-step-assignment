This Amazing Place Called Earth

We live on an awesome planet! And we're not alone - there are animals everywhere who live in unique places.

Deserts

The desert is the driest biome, and deserts receive less than 10 inches (25 centimeters) of rain per year. Desert animals learn to survive in harsh environments with very little water and large temperature changes.

Some species: fennec fox, desert tortoise, camel

Grasslands

Every continent except Antarctica has grasslands, but they can have different names in each place. For example, in Africa they are called savannas, and in South America they are called pampas.

Some species: zebra, bison, kangaroo

Ocean

Over 70 percent of Earth's surface is covered by oceans! Even though all the water is connected, scientists divide them into 5 different sections. Oceans help animals survive on Earth because they keep places from getting too hot or cold.

Some species: whale, sea otter, blobfish
